Our Roots

Where Tradition Meets the Contemporary Adda

In Rajasthan, “Hathai” is more than just a word—it is an age-old tradition. It refers to the informal daily gatherings where people sit together under the shade of a village tree or on a courtyard porch to share stories, discuss local gossip, recite poetry, and bond over cups of warm chai.

"We created Hathai to revive this beautiful essence of community, repackaged for the modern creative soul."

Set against the breathtaking backdrop of Udaipur's majestic lake palaces and rolling Aravalli hills, Hathai Udaipur is a sanctuary for slow living. Whether you are here to work on your next script, lose yourself in live acoustic melodies, or engage in deep conversations under a sunset sky, you are home.
